## Decisions made

- `next_match` returns the nearest future `planned` session only.
- `recent_matches` is limited to 4 completed sessions because the frontend overview uses it as a compact dashboard block.
- `most_popular_faction` is based on completed participations only.
- `current_leader` is ranked by `wins desc -> winrate desc -> nickname`.
- `fun_facts` are deterministic and currently generated from:
  - longest completed match
  - current leader streak
  - current top two factions by winrate
